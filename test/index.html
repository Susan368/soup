Recipe

  • 1 cup dry lentils
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions

  1. Rinse lentils under cold water and drain.
  2. In a large pot, heat olive oil over medium heat.
  3. Add onion, carrots, celery, and garlic. Cook until softened.
  4. Stir in cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  5. Add lentils and vegetable broth. Bring to a boil.
  6. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 25-30 minutes, or until lentils are tender.
  7. Remove from heat and let it sit for a few minutes before serving.

Ingredients

  • Lentils: Nutritious and hearty legumes, perfect for soups.
  • Vegetable broth: Provides flavor and depth to the soup.
  • Onion, carrots, celery, garlic: Aromatic vegetables for flavor.
  • Cumin, paprika, salt, pepper: Seasonings for added taste.
